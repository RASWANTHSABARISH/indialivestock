About The Show

Where South India's
Livestock Future
Takes Shape

The India Livestock Show is an annual event held at the Codissia Trade Fair Complex, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu — rapidly emerging as the largest and most influential livestock expo in South India.

Our focus on innovation, sustainability, and regional market trends makes ILS the definitive platform for the nation's livestock sector — a sector witnessing unprecedented growth driven by urbanisation and rising disposable income. From advanced animal husbandry equipment to veterinary biotechnology, ILS bridges breeders, farmers, exporters and policymakers under one roof.

Organised by Prompt Trade Fairs India Pvt. Ltd. — pioneers in exhibitions with 1200+ successful events across pan India over a decade.
